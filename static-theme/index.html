Welcome
to Tweenhills

Although Tweenhills is primary about stallions, we do much more and that is our strength...

Situated on 750 acres in the Gloucestershire vale, Tweenhills has long been regarded as one of the finest stock farms. Nestling between the Malvern Hills and the River Severn, stock thrives on the permanent pasture. The paddocks are sheltered by ancient hedgerows and magnificent oaks, and are farmed in strict rotation with our resident red Aberdeen Angus suckler herd. During the winter months the farm is grazed extensively by sheep.
